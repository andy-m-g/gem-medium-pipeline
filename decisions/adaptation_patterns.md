# Adaptation Patterns

This file captures recurring medium-adaptation patterns extracted from the
preserved local BHI and CIM evidence.

Purpose:

- preserve recurring curator reasoning in explicit tabular form
- keep these patterns visible and reviewable
- support future recipe training and fixture creation without converting them
  into automatic medium or adapt decisions

Boundaries:

- These patterns are not an automatic policy engine.
- `uptake at limit` and related diagnostics remain evidence, not decisions.
- Medium revision remains separate from reconstruction or adapt-side
  supplementation.
